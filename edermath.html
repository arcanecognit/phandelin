Adventurer retired to a cottage beside an apple orchard. Fit, silver-haired half-elf well over a hundred years old. Daran is a fighter who served as a marshal and herlad for many years in the lands of the Dragon Coast, far to the Southeast. Retired at home

Member of the Order of the Guantlet, a devout and vigilant group that seeks to protect others from the depredations of evildoers. The order is always vigilant, ready to smite evil, enforce justice, and enact retribution against any who try to subjugate or harm others

Daran is concerned about the redbrands, and he would like to see a group of adventurers teach the ruffians a lesson. "It's time someone took a stand against Glasstaff." "The rabble hang around the Sleeping Giant tap house, but their main hideout is under Tresendar Manor

Quest: Old Owl Trouble Daran has heard stories from prospectors in the hills northeast of Phandelin that someone is digging around in the ruins known as Old Owl Well. More disturbingly, several propspectors have reported being chased from the area by undead. He asks them to visit the site a two day march northeast. Find out who's there and what they're up to. He knows that the ruins are an old watchtower of an ancient magical empire known as Netheril, and he worries that dangerous magic might be dormant there.
